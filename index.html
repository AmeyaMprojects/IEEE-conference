About NQComp

The International Conference on "Next-Gen Quantum Computing: Algorithms, Security, and Beyond" (NQComp-2026), scheduled for March 5th and 6th, 2026 at Manipal Institute of Technology, Bengaluru, aims to be a premier platform for academic scholars, researchers, and industry professionals to explore the latest advancements in quantum computing. As quantum computing continues to revolutionize various fields, this conference will focus on cutting-edge developments in quantum algorithms, security, and their diverse applications.


With quantum computing poised to redefine industries such as cryptography, artificial intelligence, and data science, NQComp-2026 will provide a collaborative environment to exchange ideas, present novel research, and discuss future trends in this transformative technology. The conference will bring together experts from academia, research labs, and industry to address current challenges and discuss opportunities for innovation and application in quantum computing.
